1. Project Overview

TourDesk is a Travel Content Management System (TCMS) designed for travel companies to manage destinations, hotels, hotel pricing, and marketing content from a single dashboard while providing customers with a simple and responsive hotel discovery website.

The system focuses on simplifying daily operations, reducing repetitive manual work, organizing tourism data, and generating professional marketing content with minimal effort.

2. Vision

To become a simple, modern, and efficient platform that enables travel companies to manage all tourism content from one place instead of relying on spreadsheets, manual editing, and repetitive social media posts.

The first version focuses on hotel management and marketing content generation, while the architecture is designed to support future expansion.

3. Problem Statement

Today, travel companies typically:

Store hotel information manually.
Receive monthly pricing updates from suppliers.
Rewrite pricing posts every month.
Keep hotel information scattered.
Spend unnecessary time preparing marketing content.
Lack a central platform to organize tourism data.

This results in:

Wasted time.
Higher chance of human errors.
Difficult content management.
Poor customer browsing experience.
4. Solution

TourDesk provides a centralized dashboard that allows the company owner to:

Manage cities.
Manage hotels.
Manage hotel pricing.
Manage room types.
Manage meal plans.
Manage hotel features.
Manage company information.
Upload hotel images.
Generate marketing posts automatically.
Publish hotel data to the customer website.

Customers can simply browse hotels, filter results, and contact the company directly through WhatsApp without creating accounts or making online payments.

8. Out of Scope (Version 1)

The following features are intentionally excluded:

Online booking.
Online payments.
Customer accounts.
Employee management.
Reports & Analytics.
Supplier management.
Reservation management.
Notifications.
Email marketing.
Multi-company support.

These may be considered in future versions.

9. Product Principles

Every feature in TourDesk must follow these principles:

Simplicity First

The interface should remain simple and easy to use.

Minimize Clicks

The dashboard should reduce the number of clicks required to complete repetitive tasks.

Single Source of Truth

All information should be managed from one centralized dashboard.

No Manual Repetition

The system should automate repetitive content whenever possible.

Mobile Friendly

The system must work smoothly on both desktop and mobile devices.

Future Ready

The architecture should support future migration to ASP.NET Core and SQL Server without requiring product redesign.

10. Success Metrics

The product will be considered successful if:

Monthly pricing updates become significantly faster.
Marketing posts can be generated automatically.
Hotel information remains organized.
Customers can easily discover hotels.
The owner can manage all content from one place.
